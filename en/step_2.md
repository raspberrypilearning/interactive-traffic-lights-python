## Connect the components

--- task ---

- Raspberry Pi
- 3x LEDs and resistors
- Button
- Buzzer
- Breadboard
- Jumper wires

Wire up your components to the following GPIO pins:

| Component | GPIO pin |
| --------- | :------: |
| Button    | 21       |
| Red LED   | 25       |
| Amber LED | 28       |
| Green LED | 27       |
| Buzzer    | 15       |
