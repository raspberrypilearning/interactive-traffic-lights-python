## Connect the components

\--- task \---

- Raspberry Pi
- 3x LEDs und Widerstände
- Taster
- Summer
- Steckbrett
- Verbindungsdrähte

Wire up your components to the following GPIO pins:

| Komponente | GPIO-Stift |
| ---------- |:----------:|
| Taster     |     21     |
| Rote LED   |     25     |
| Gelbe LED  |     28     |
| Grüne LED  |     27     |
| Summer     |     15     |
